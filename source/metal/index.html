Hëävy Mëtälizr
Welcome to Heavy Metalizer. Have you every had some text that you needed to rock sooo much harder than it was but you just couldn't be bothered to figure out how to type a Umlaut? It happens to all of us right? Well now you can just turn to the Heavy Metalizr to do it for you. Enter some text and smell the glove to awesomeize your text.

Heavy Metalizr is also available as a node.js npm for when you need to make things rock asynchronously.
